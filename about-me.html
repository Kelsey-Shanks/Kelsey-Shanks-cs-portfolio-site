About Kelsey Shanks

Kelsey Shanks attends Oregon State University as a post-baccalaureate e-campus computer science student. She is originally from Edmond, Oklahoma, but she currently lives in Okinawa, Japan for her husband's job on base. She has a Bachelor of Arts in Music from Oklahoma Christian University, a Master of Music in Instrumental Performance (Flute) degree from Oklahoma City University, and will graduate from Oregon State University in June 2026 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

Careers of Interest:

  • Music

    Due to the nature of Kelsey's previous education, she is particularly interested in developing software that is connected to music education, music entertainment, or music technology. However, she has a growing interest in cybersecurity and user interface design due to her coursework at Oregon State University and has taken courses to learn more about those facets of computer science.

  • Cybersecurity

    In addition to music, Kelsey is also interested in careers in cybersecurity. She is set to take Intro to Computer Networks and Intro to Security as electives to delve deeper into the subject as well as take the Security+ certification exam in the next few months.

  • User Interface Design

    Her interest also extends to user interface design after taking Web Development and Intro to Usability Engineering. From those courses, she's worked with React.js, REST API, Node.js, Figma, HTML, and UX research to design a product.

  • Healthcare Software Design

    From her time in high school attending Francis Tuttle Bioscience and Medicine Academy to now, Kelsey has always had a keen interest in medicine and healthcare. Due to this, she conducted her usability project on making a personal health portal that interfaced with wearable biometric devices (e.g. Garmin watches).
